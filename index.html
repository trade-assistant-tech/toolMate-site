What it does

Every step handled.
You just approve.

From first message to repeat booking, ToolMate manages the admin at every stage. Nothing reaches a customer without your sign-off.

1. Enquiry

A customer messages you on WhatsApp, email, SMS, or Instagram. ToolMate picks it up instantly and drafts a professional reply for your approval, whether it's 9 am or 11 pm.

  • •Qualifies the lead so you only spend time on real jobs
  • •Replies in your tone of voice, not a generic bot
  • •Handles emergency and out-of-hours calls

2. Quote

ToolMate drafts an itemised quote based on the unique job details. You review it, tweak it if needed, and approve. Nothing goes out without your sign-off.

  • •Pulls in live supplier pricing for materials
  • •Chases unanswered quotes automatically in your voice
  • •Flags edge cases that need your personal judgement

3. Schedule

ToolMate finds an open slot in your calendar, proposes it to the customer, and books the job once you confirm. No back-and-forth texts to arrange a date.

  • •Groups nearby jobs to cut travel time between sites
  • •Sends the customer a booking confirmation instantly
  • •Reschedules automatically if your diary changes

4. Job Execution

You focus on the work. ToolMate handles everything around it. The customer gets a reminder before you arrive, and you get a briefing so nothing is missed on site.

  • •Morning summary with site notes and materials needed
  • •Customer gets an arrival-window notification
  • •Send photos or voice notes via WhatsApp to be logged against the job

5. Invoice & Payment

Job done? ToolMate sends the invoice automatically. If payment doesn't arrive, it chases politely at 7, 14, and 30 days so you don't have to.

  • •Invoice sent on job completion
  • •Automated payment reminders at 7, 14, and 30 days
  • •Full customer payment history tracked

6. Reviews & Repeat Work

After every successful job, ToolMate sends a review request to the customer. It also keeps a full history of every customer and proactively reaches out when seasonal or recurring work is due.

  • •Automatic post-job review requests
  • •Customer history: jobs done, addresses, payment behaviour
  • •Proactive outreach for seasonal and recurring work
